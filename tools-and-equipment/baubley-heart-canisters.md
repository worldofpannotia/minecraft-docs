## Tools & Equipment
### Baubley Heart Canisters

* **Red Hearts**: 5% chance to drop from any hostile mob
* **Yellow Hearts**: 100% drop from 'boss' entities
* **Green Hearts**: 100% drop from the dragon
* **Blue Hearts**: 100% drop from evokers
